Privacy Policy for CybeLink-5

1. Data Collection 
Decentralized Nature: CybeLink-5 is a decentralized application (dApp) built on the Solana blockchain.

No Personal Information: We do NOT collect names, emails, phone numbers, or physical addresses.

Wallet Addresses: We only interact with your public wallet address to verify $SOL and $SKR balances and process in-game transactions.

No Private Keys: We NEVER have access to your private keys or seed phrases.

2. Data Usage 
The public data provided by the Solana blockchain is used solely to:

Display Cryptocurrency Information: Provide real-time public market data for $SOL and $SKR.

3. Third-Party Services
We use standard Solana RPC providers to interact with the blockchain. These services may log public transaction data as per their own privacy policies.

4. Data Security
Since we do not store your data on private servers, your security is tied directly to the security of the Solana blockchain and your chosen wallet provider (e.g., Phantom, Solflare).
