Privacy Policy for CybeLink-5
Last Updated: March 18, 2026

1. Decentralized Nature
CybeLink-5 is a decentralized application (dApp) built on the Solana Blockchain. By design, the application interacts directly with the blockchain via smart contracts and does not utilize a centralized user database.

2. Data Collection & No PII
We operate on a Zero-Knowledge principle regarding personal identity:

No Personal Identifiable Information (PII): We do NOT collect, store, or solicit names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical locations.

Public Wallet Addresses: We only interact with your public wallet address to verify $SOL and $SKR balances and facilitate on-chain game mechanics.

No Private Keys: We NEVER have access to, nor do we ever request, your private keys or seed phrases. Your security is managed by your chosen wallet provider (e.g., Phantom, Solflare).

3. Data Usage
Publicly available on-chain data is used strictly to:

Verify Assets: To confirm ownership of $SOL/$SKR for premium features (AI Hints).

Game Logic: To process game-related transactions and state updates.

Market Data: To display real-time public market information for supported tokens.

4. Third-Party Services
We utilize standard Solana RPC providers (e.g., Helius, Triton, or QuickNode) to communicate with the blockchain. These providers may log public transaction hash data according to their own privacy policies. We do not control these third-party logs.

5. Cookies & Local Storage
We do not use tracking cookies. App preferences (e.g., language, haptics) are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage and are never transmitted to our servers.
